01 — The person behind the code
About me

I'm a third-year Computer Science undergrad at IIT Goa, and honestly my two favorite states of mind are "mid-hackathon panic" and "just solved a CF problem I'd been stuck on for an hour." Both feel the same, weirdly.

I care a lot about building things that actually work — not just demo well. On EventFlow, that meant obsessing over whether the async job queue would survive real load, not just whether the UI looked nice. I'd rather ship something a little less shiny that doesn't fall over.

Right now I'm solo on EngageIQ for the SBI Hackathon at GFF 2026 — an agentic AI system for digital banking. Solo means no one to blame when something breaks at 2am, but also no one to argue with about architecture, so, trade-offs.

When I'm not at a keyboard: I run Qalam, IIT Goa's literary club, and I've represented IIT Goa in athletics at Inter-IIT for two years straight. Turns out sprinting and debugging under time pressure use the same part of my brain.
